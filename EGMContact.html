EG Maintenance — A Movement for a Healthier Salem

EG Maintenance was built on a simple belief: a city thrives when its people do.

We are more than a service company — we are a self-sustaining community engine dedicated to restoring pride, beauty, and health across Salem.

Every lawn we revive, every park we repair, and every bag of litter we remove is part of a bigger mission: to give back more than we take.

Our business model is designed with intention. The work we do funds the work we give. A portion of every project, every shirt sold, and every supporter who joins our movement helps fuel cleanup efforts, park repairs, community projects, and environmental restoration across the city we love.

We are not waiting for change — we are building it with our own hands.

And we are inviting Salem to build it with us.

When residents, visitors, and local businesses unite behind a shared purpose, something powerful happens: a cleaner, safer, healthier Salem becomes possible — and unstoppable.

This isn’t just maintenance. This is momentum. This is community. This is EGM.

Join the movement. Help us keep Salem shining.
